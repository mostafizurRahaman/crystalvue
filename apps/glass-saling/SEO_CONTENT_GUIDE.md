## General Settings

### Site Title

**Purpose:** The main title of your website that appears in browser tabs and
search results.

**Best Practices:**

- Keep it concise (50-60 characters recommended)
- Include your primary brand name
- Include location if relevant (e.g., "Qatar")
- Use proper capitalization
- Avoid special characters that may not display correctly

**Current Value:** `Glass Expert Qatar` _(Note: Typo - should be "Qatar")_

**Recommended:** `Glass Expert Qatar` or
`Glass Expert Qatar | Premium Glass Solutions`

**Character Limit:** 60 characters (optimal for search engines)

---

### Site Description

**Purpose:** A brief overview of your business that appears in search results
and social media shares.

**Best Practices:**

- Write 150-160 characters (optimal for search snippets)
- Include primary services and location
- Use natural, compelling language
- Include a call-to-action if space allows
- Focus on what makes you unique

**Current Value:**

```
Glass Expert Qatar is a leading glass supplier and installation company in Qatar, offering tempered, laminated, shower glass, partitions, sliding doors, mirrors, and custom glass solutions. We provide durable, safe, and elegant glass fabrication, delivery, and installation services for residential and commercial projects across Qatar.
```

**Character Count:** 255 characters _(Too long - should be 160 max)_

**Recommended (160 characters):**

```
Glass Expert Qatar offers premium tempered glass, laminated glass, partitions, mirrors, and custom installation services. Trusted glass supplier for residential and commercial projects in Qatar.
```

---

### Logo Image

**Purpose:** Your company logo displayed throughout the website.

**Best Practices:**

- Use high-resolution image (minimum 200x200px, recommended 400x400px)
- Use PNG format with transparent background (preferred)
- Ensure logo is clear and readable at small sizes
- File size should be under 500KB for fast loading
- Include proper ALT text: "Glass Expert Qatar Logo"

**Technical Requirements:**

- Formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, SVG
- Recommended dimensions: 400x400px (square) or 800x200px (wide)
- Max file size: 500KB

---

### Favicon Image

**Purpose:** Small icon displayed in browser tabs, bookmarks, and mobile home
screens.

**Best Practices:**

- Use square image (16x16px minimum, 32x32px recommended)
- Simple, recognizable design
- High contrast for visibility
- PNG or ICO format
- Should work at very small sizes

**Technical Requirements:**

- Formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, SVG, ICO
- Recommended dimensions: 32x32px or 64x64px
- Max file size: 50KB

---

### Meta Image (OG Image)

**Purpose:** Image displayed when your website is shared on social media
(Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.).

**Best Practices:**

- Use high-quality image (1200x630px recommended)
- Include your logo and key messaging
- Make it visually appealing and brand-consistent
- Test how it looks on different social platforms
- Include text that's readable at small sizes

**Technical Requirements:**

- Formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP
- Recommended dimensions: 1200x630px (1.91:1 ratio)
- Max file size: 1MB
- Include ALT text: "Glass Expert Qatar - Premium Glass Solutions"

## SEO Settings

### SEO Meta Title

**Purpose:** The title that appears in search engine results (SERP). This is the
most important SEO element.

**Best Practices:**

- **Optimal length: 50-60 characters** (search engines truncate after ~60)
- Include primary keyword at the beginning
- Include location (Qatar) if targeting local searches
- Include brand name
- Make it compelling and click-worthy
- Use pipe (|) to separate elements
- Avoid keyword stuffing

**Current Value:**

```
Glass Expert Qatar | Premium Glass Supplier & Installation Company in Qatar
```

**Character Count:** 96 characters _(Too long - should be 60-75 max)_

**Recommended (72 characters):**

```
Glass Expert Qatar | Premium Glass Supplier & Installation
```

**Alternative (65 characters):**

```
Premium Glass Supplier Qatar | Glass Expert Qatar
```

**Character Limit:** 60-75 characters (optimal for search engines)

---

### SEO Meta Description

**Purpose:** The description that appears under the title in search results.
This influences click-through rates.

**Best Practices:**

- **Optimal length: 150-160 characters** (search engines truncate after ~160)
- Include primary keywords naturally
- Write compelling, action-oriented copy
- Include a call-to-action
- Make it unique and relevant
- Avoid duplicate descriptions across pages

**Current Value:**

```
Glass Expert Qatar offers high-quality tempered glass, laminated glass, glass partitions, mirrors, shower glass, and custom glass installation services across Qatar. Reliable, affordable, and trusted glass supplier for residential and commercial projects.
```

**Character Count:** 255 characters _(Too long - should be 160 max)_

**Recommended (158 characters):**

```
Glass Expert Qatar offers premium tempered glass, laminated glass, partitions, mirrors, and custom installation. Trusted glass supplier for residential and commercial projects in Qatar.
```

**Alternative (155 characters):**

```
Premium glass supplier in Qatar. Tempered glass, laminated glass, partitions, mirrors, and custom installation services. Reliable, affordable solutions for residential and commercial projects.
```

**Character Limit:** 150-160 characters (optimal for search engines)

---

### SEO Keywords

**Purpose:** Keywords help search engines understand your content focus. While
less important than before, they still provide context.

**Best Practices:**

- Use 10-15 relevant keywords maximum
- Include primary and secondary keywords
- Include location-based keywords (Qatar, Doha)
- Use long-tail keywords (3-4 word phrases)
- Separate keywords with commas
- Focus on what customers actually search for
- Include variations and synonyms

**Current Value:**

```
glass supplier qatar, glass company qatar, glass installation qatar, tempered glass qatar, laminated glass qatar, glass partition qatar, shower glass qatar, custom glass qatar, mirror supplier qatar, sliding glass door qatar, glass works qatar, glass company doha, glass installation doha
```

**Analysis:**

- ✅ Good coverage of primary keywords
- ✅ Includes location variations (Qatar, Doha)
- ✅ Includes service-specific keywords
- ✅ Good mix of short and long-tail keywords

**Recommended (Enhanced):**

```
glass supplier qatar, glass company qatar, glass installation qatar, tempered glass qatar, laminated glass qatar, glass partition qatar, shower glass qatar, custom glass qatar, mirror supplier qatar, sliding glass door qatar, glass works qatar, glass company doha, glass installation doha, glass fabrication qatar, commercial glass qatar, residential glass qatar, glass repair qatar
```

**Keyword Research Tips:**

1. Use Google Keyword Planner
2. Check Google Autocomplete suggestions
3. Analyze competitor keywords
4. Use long-tail keywords (3-4 words)
5. Include location + service combinations
